Last updated: February 03, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the AC Remote mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Use

Our AC Remote application is designed to function primarily as a hardware controller (via Infrared or Wi-Fi). We prioritize user privacy and minimize data collection:

  • Usage Data: We may collect basic technical information such as your device model and OS version to optimize IR signal performance and app stability.
  • Infrared (IR) Permissions: The app requires access to your device's IR blaster to send commands to your air conditioning unit.
  • Network/Wi-Fi Permissions: Required only if your AC unit is controlled via a local network. We do not track your browsing history or external network traffic.

2. Third-Party Services

To keep the application free, we may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Used for technical operations and app stability on Android.
  • AdMob / Third-Party Ads: Used to display advertisements. These providers may use cookies or device identifiers to show ads based on your interests.

3. Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. This app does not use "cookies" directly, but third-party ad providers may use them to enhance ad targeting.
