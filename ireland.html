Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 October 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ireland Live TV (“the App”, “we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you use the App.

1) Summary

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless you voluntarily contact us.
  • The App opens and embeds third-party TV streams and websites. Those third parties may process data under their own policies.

2) Information We Process

a) Information you provide

If you contact us by email, we will receive your message and address to respond to your request.

b) Information collected automatically

The App makes standard network requests to play streams and open broadcaster pages. Our App itself does not store or profile you. Your device and OS (e.g., Android/iOS) may create basic technical logs for reliability and security.

Note: If we introduce analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs, we will update this Policy and provide in-app disclosures and controls.

3) Third-Party Content & Links

The App may open or embed content from third-party broadcasters (e.g., their official sites or players). Your use of those services is governed by their own terms and privacy policies. Please review them before using those services.

4) Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

5) Data Retention

We retain emails you send us only for as long as necessary to handle your request and to comply with legal obligations.

6) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7) International Users

Your communications with us may be processed in the European Economic Area (EEA) and/or other countries. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

8) Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., EEA/UK), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the new effective date above and, where required, notify you in the App.
