Privacy Policy for Safe Step

Safe Step respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the app may collect, use, store, and protect information when you use Safe Step.

1. Information You Provide

Safe Step may process content that you choose to provide inside the app, such as:

  • Text you paste into the app
  • Images or screenshots you select
  • Documents or PDF files you import
  • Content captured using your device camera

This information is used only to provide the app’s analysis and related features.

2. Local Processing

Some app features may process content locally on your device. Depending on the version of the app and the specific functionality being used, certain analysis tasks may also involve external services or third-party tools.

Safe Step is designed to minimize unnecessary data handling whenever possible.

3. Stored Information

Safe Step may store certain information locally on your device, including:

  • Analysis history
  • User preferences and settings
  • Task or status labels you assign to analyzed items

This information is stored to improve usability and allow you to revisit previous results.

4. Advertising

Safe Step may display advertisements through third-party advertising providers such as AdMob. These providers may collect certain device and usage data in accordance with their own privacy practices.

Advertising partners may use identifiers, device information, and interaction data to deliver and measure ads.

5. Analytics and Diagnostics

Safe Step may collect limited technical or diagnostic information to help improve performance, stability, and user experience. This may include app usage events, crash data, and general interaction information.

6. Permissions

Safe Step may request access to certain device features when needed for app functionality, such as:

  • Camera access for capturing images
  • Storage or file access for importing documents and media
  • Internet access for ads or supported online features

Permissions are used only for their intended app functions.

7. Third-Party Services

Safe Step may rely on third-party services for certain features, including advertising, text recognition, analytics, or other technical functions. These third parties may process information according to their own privacy policies.

8. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information handled by the app. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. Children’s Privacy

Safe Step is not intended for children. The app does not knowingly target or collect personal information from children.

10. Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time. You may also clear locally stored app data through your device settings or by using any in-app history or reset options that may be available.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Safe Step after any changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the app store listing or the contact methods made available through the app distribution platform.
