1. Controller

SMA-Apps

The controller determines the purposes and means of processing related to LED Strip Remote.

2. Data We Process

  • Technical data: device model, OS version, language, country, time zone, device identifiers, Advertising ID.
  • Usage data: basic screen interactions and events (e.g., connect to device, send color).
  • Network: IP address (for network communications) and settings you configure locally (e.g., host/port for Wi-Fi controller). We do not send these settings to our servers; they are used on-device to communicate with your controller.
  • Advertising (AdMob): advertising identifiers, ad impressions, performance metrics and, if you allow, personalization data.
  • Support (if you contact us): name, email, and the information you provide.

Location: the app may request permissions that Android associates with “location” to scan for Bluetooth LE devices. We do not access or store your GPS location; the permission is a technical requirement on certain Android versions.

3. Permissions

  • BLUETOOTH / BLUETOOTH_SCAN / BLUETOOTH_CONNECT: discover, connect and control compatible BLE devices.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: required by Android for BLE scanning; we do not use GPS.
  • INTERNET: show ads (AdMob) and, if you configure Wi-Fi control, communicate with your controller (UDP/HTTP).

4. Purposes & Legal Basis

  • App functionality (contract/service): control LED strips via BLE/Wi-Fi/IR; local preferences.
  • Improvement & diagnostics (legitimate interest): stability and aggregate, non-identifiable usage metrics.
  • Advertising (consent / legitimate interest depending on region): display AdMob ads and measure performance.
  • Support (consent): respond to your requests.
