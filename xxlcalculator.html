Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Calculator XXL (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use the App. We aim to keep the App simple and accessible. The App itself does not require you to create an account or enter personal details.

1) What We Collect

The App does not collect personal information directly from you.

However, because the App displays ads, our advertising partner may collect certain data automatically to serve and measure ads:

  • Advertising data: ad impressions, clicks, performance metrics;
  • Device and usage data: device model, OS version, language, network information, coarse location (approximate), IP address;
  • Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (AAID) or similar identifiers used for advertising and analytics.

Advertising partner: Google AdMob.

2) How We Use Information

  • To display, optimize, and measure in-app advertising;
  • To keep the App reliable and prevent fraud/abuse;
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3) Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect and process data as described in Google’s policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses information from apps: partner-sites
  • Ads Settings / personalization controls: adssettings.google.com

Ad data may be shared with ad measurement and anti-fraud partners as needed for legitimate purposes (e.g., frequency capping, aggregated reporting, detecting invalid activity).

4) Legal Bases (EEA/UK users)

Where required, AdMob may seek your consent for personalized ads. If you do not consent, ads may still appear but will be non-personalized (contextual). You can withdraw or change choices via the in-app prompts (where available) or device settings.

5) Your Choices & Controls

  • Reset / limit ads tracking: On Android, you can reset or limit the Advertising ID in device Settings > Privacy > Ads (path may vary).
  • Ads personalization: Manage on adssettings.google.com.
  • Consent: If a consent prompt is shown, use it to choose personalized or non-personalized ads.

6) Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age where parental consent is required in your region (e.g., under 13 in the U.S.). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7) Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Ad-related data is retained by Google and its partners according to their policies. See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

8) Security

We rely on Google’s infrastructure and standard industry practices to help protect data processed for advertising and analytics.

9) International Transfers

Advertising partners may process data on servers located in different countries. Google describes applicable transfer mechanisms in its policies and documentation.

10) Your Rights

Depending on your region (e.g., EEA/UK/California), you may have rights such as access, deletion, correction, objection, or portability regarding personal data processed for ads. To exercise these, use the controls listed above or contact us and we will coordinate with our partners as needed.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new version here and update the “Effective date”. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
